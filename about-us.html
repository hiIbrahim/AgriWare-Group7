AgriWare

Agri Ware is the first modern fertilizer and agricultural supply warehouse serving farmers in Oddar Meanchey Province, with a strong commitment to product quality. Agri Ware also collaborates with Davy Rice Warehouse to ensure the purchase of all farmers' produce.
